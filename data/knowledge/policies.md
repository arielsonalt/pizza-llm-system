## 1. General Information

**Pizza Chop** is a food service establishment specializing in handcrafted pizzas for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery.
All policies below apply to orders placed via in-store, phone, website, mobile apps, chatbots, or third-party platforms, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

---

## 2. Ordering Policy

### 2.1 Accepted Ordering Channels

- In-store counter
- Phone orders
- Official website / app
- Messaging platforms (e.g., WhatsApp, chat assistants)
- Authorized third-party delivery platforms

### 2.2 Order Confirmation

- An order is considered **confirmed** only after:
  - Explicit customer confirmation **and**
  - Successful payment authorization or approval for cash on delivery (COD)
- Customers are responsible for reviewing:
  - Pizza flavors
  - Size
  - Crust type
  - Extras
  - Delivery address and contact information

### 2.3 Order Modifications

- Orders can be modified **only before preparation begins**
- Once preparation starts, changes may not be possible
- Modifications may affect preparation time and final price

---

## 3. Pricing Policy

### 3.1 Prices

- All prices are displayed in local currency
- Prices may vary by:
  - Pizza size
  - Location
  - Delivery zone
  - Promotional campaigns
- Prices are subject to change without prior notice

### 3.2 Taxes and Fees

- Prices may include or exclude applicable taxes depending on local regulations
- Delivery fees are calculated based on distance and demand
- Service fees may apply on third-party platforms

## 4. Payment Policy

### 4.1 Accepted Payment Methods

- Credit cards
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (where available)
- Cash on delivery (COD)
- Online payment links

### 4.2 Payment Security

- All online payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment providers
- Pizza Chop does not store full card details

### 4.3 Failed or Rejected Payments

- Orders with failed payment authorization will not be processed
- Reattempts may be required before confirmation

## 5. Preparation & Cooking Policy

### 5.1 Preparation Standards

- All pizzas are prepared fresh upon order
- Ingredients are stored and handled following food safety regulations
- Standard crust contains gluten unless otherwise specified

### 5.2 Customization

- Customers may customize pizzas with:
  - Extra toppings
  - Ingredient removal
  - Half-and-half flavors (where available)
- Customizations may incur additional costs

## 6. Allergen & Dietary Policy

### 6.1 Allergen Disclosure

- Allergen information is provided in `allergens.md`
- Common allergens include:
  - Gluten (wheat)
  - Milk/dairy
  - Egg
  - Fish
  - Shellfish
  - Soy (possible in processed meats and sauces)

### 6.2 Cross-Contamination Disclaimer

- Our kitchen handles multiple allergens
- Cross-contamination **cannot be fully eliminated**
- Pizza Chop does not guarantee allergen-free meals

### 6.3 Special Diets

- Vegetarian options available
- Vegan, lactose-free, or gluten-free options depend on availability and location
- Customers must inform dietary needs at the time of ordering

---

## 7. Delivery Policy

### 7.1 Delivery Areas

- Delivery is limited to predefined service zones
- Orders outside delivery zones may be declined

### 7.2 Delivery Time Estimates

- Delivery times are estimates and not guarantees
- Delays may occur due to:
  - Weather
  - Traffic
  - High demand
  - Operational issues

### 7.3 Failed Delivery

Delivery may be considered failed if:

- Customer is unreachable
- Incorrect address provided
- Customer refuses to accept the order

In such cases:

- No refund is guaranteed
- Order may be returned or discarded

---

## 8. Pickup Policy

### 8.1 Pickup Window

- Orders should be collected within **30 minutes** of readiness
- Delayed pickup may affect food quality

### 8.2 Responsibility

- Pizza Chop is not responsible for quality degradation due to late pickup

## 9. Cancellation Policy

### 9.1 Customer-Initiated Cancellation

- Allowed only **before preparation begins**
- Once preparation starts, cancellations are generally not refundable

### 9.2 Store-Initiated Cancellation

Pizza Chop reserves the right to cancel orders due to:

- Ingredient unavailability
- Technical issues
- Payment problems
- Force majeure events

Refunds will be issued when applicable.
